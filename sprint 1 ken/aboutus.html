A fashion company that designers and users can access range of stylish clothing and accessories.


Who we are


Stylix is a fashion web application designed to connect users with personal designers to curate wardrobes that reflect individuality, culture, and confidence.
Users to discover designers, build curated wardrobes, and receive personalized styling experiences. Designers to showcase their creativity, gain exposure, and connect with clients who value their craft.

Our goals


Stylix operates through a dual-dashboard system: A User Dashboard for wardrobe curation, designer booking, and style management. A Designer Dashboard for portfolio showcasing, client interaction, and brand growth.
